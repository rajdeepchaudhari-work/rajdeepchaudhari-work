● LIVE EAGERHQ · 01

Voxlit

macOS · Voice dictation + AI Agent

Press a hotkey, speak, and the transcript lands in the focused app. Runs locally via bundled whisper.cpp — no account, no subscription, no telemetry. Say "Hey Voxlit" for 25+ built-in skills: draft an email, write a commit message, summarise a page.

§ 04

The Human

Who's behind the page

I grew up taking things apart to see what was inside, and I never really grew out of it — just swapped the screwdriver for a terminal.

I moved from India to London for university in 2023. Since then I've been quietly assembling a strange but coherent life — a retail job, a union role, a mentoring gig, a software studio on the side, and a Computer Science degree holding it all together. It shouldn't make sense on paper, but it does to me.

I'm drawn to cybersecurity, especially the GRC side — policy, controls, risk, the bits where the human and the machine have to agree on the rules. It rhymes with the union rep work, which is funny.

Outside all that, I like making small tools that respect the person using them — no tracking, no dark patterns, no "we've updated our terms" emails. If you use Voxlit or Patchbay, no server of mine knows about it.
